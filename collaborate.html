Industry Collaboration at THRIVE

A defining feature of THRIVE is its commitment to meaningful, reciprocal collaboration with industry partners. Across her career, Dr Faith Matcham has worked with organisations ranging from digital health start-ups to global pharmaceutical companies, providing expertise in behaviour change, remote monitoring, intervention design, user engagement, and real-world evaluation.

How We Collaborate

THRIVE builds on this foundation by working closely with companies developing digital therapeutics, wearable technologies, sensing systems, health platforms, and AI-enabled tools. Our collaborations span:

  • Co-design of digital health products
  • Evaluation of feasibility and acceptability
  • Behavioural science for engagement
  • End-to-end research support
  • Implementation science approaches
  • User testing and impact assessment

Our industry partners value THRIVE's ability to combine human-centred design, rigorous scientific methods, and practical insight into how people engage with technology in their daily lives. We specialise in bridging the gap between innovative ideas and usable, effective tools that make a genuine difference to health and wellbeing.

If you are an industry collaborator interested in evidence-based development, user experience refinement, or robust evaluation of digital health products, THRIVE offers a uniquely interdisciplinary and impact-focused partnership model.
