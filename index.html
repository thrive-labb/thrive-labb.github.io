GetDigital Webinar Series

Predicting mood from wearables and smartphones in depression

With Faith Matcham, University of Sussex, UK

Speaker: Faith Matcham (United Kingdom)
Date: March 25, 2026
Time: 16.00 CET
Format: Online Webinar
Series: ECNP Digital Health Applied to Clinical Research

Understanding and anticipating short-term mood changes in Major Depressive Disorder (MDD) is a critical step toward precision psychiatry. This talk presents work from the RADAR-MDD initiative, which harnesses multimodal digital data—passive sensing from wearables and smartphones, combined with ecological momentary assessments—to explore how everyday behaviours and experiences shape mood dynamics.

The approach integrates advanced time-series modelling and machine learning to move beyond relapse prediction toward real-time forecasting of daily mood states, with the ultimate goal of embedding predictive tools into digital health platforms to support timely, individualised care.
